How this works

Each scenario presents a stream of raw log events from a SIEM. Some events belong to a single attack chain; the rest are routine activity (noise). Click each log line you believe is part of the attack, then submit to see how your correlation compares to the analyst's notes.

Your task is the same one a SOC analyst faces during alert triage: pivot on identity, host, IP, and time to find what hangs together.
